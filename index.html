    About Us

    Welcome to the Energy and Water Sector Education and Training Authority (EWSETA), one of South Africa's esteemed SETAs. As a specialized institution established under Section 9 of the Skills Development Act (SDA), 97 of 1998, EWSETA plays a pivotal role in the development and advancement of the Energy and Water sector within the country. We proudly report to the Honourable Minister of Higher Education, Science and Technology, working closely with key stakeholders to drive progress and excellence in the industry.

    EWSETA's primary mission is to foster a skilled and empowered workforce that meets the evolving needs of the Energy and Water sector in South Africa. Our mandate encompasses a comprehensive range of responsibilities, including facilitating education, training, and skills development initiatives, as well as promoting the recognition of prior learning and qualifications within the industry.

    Services

    We are actively engaged in two crucial sectors:
    South Africa's Water Sector and South Africa's Energy Sector.
    These sectors play a vital role in ensuring the well-being and prosperity of our nation.

    SA's Water Sector

    Our primary focus within the water sector is to ensure that every citizen of South Africa has access to clean and safe water. We are dedicated to implementing sustainable water management practices, optimizing water distribution systems, and safeguarding water sources to meet the growing demands of our population.


    Through continuous monitoring and maintenance of water infrastructure, we strive to minimize water wastage, prevent pollution, and enhance the overall quality of water resources. By promoting awareness and education about water conservation, we aim to create a culture of responsible water usage across the country.

    Energy Sector

    In the energy sector, our objective is to provide reliable and affordable energy to support South Africa's economic growth and development. We are committed to diversifying our energy sources, reducing dependence on fossil fuels, and embracing renewable energy alternatives.


    Additionally, we prioritize the expansion and modernization of our energy infrastructure, ensuring efficient transmission and distribution of electricity to meet the increasing energy demands of industries, businesses, and households.

    Available Careers



    A Career in Energy

    Energy careers encompass a wide range of roles and opportunities within the energy sector. These careers involve the production, distribution, and management of energy resources such as electricity, oil, gas, and renewable energy sources. Professionals in this field work on energy exploration, generation, transmission, and storage, as well as energy efficiency, policy, and sustainability. They contribute to ensuring a reliable and sustainable energy supply for industries, communities, and individuals.

    I'am interested!

    Water Management

    Water careers revolve around the management, conservation, and distribution of water resources. Professionals in this field work on water treatment, wastewater management, water quality assessment, hydrology, irrigation, and water resource planning. They play a crucial role in ensuring the availability of clean and safe water for domestic, industrial, and agricultural purposes, while also addressing water scarcity, pollution, and sustainable water management practices.

    This is for me!

    Artisan Careers

    Artisan careers are centered around skilled craftsmanship and working with one's hands. These careers involve various trades, such as carpentry, plumbing, electrical work, welding, masonry, and automotive repair. Artisans possess specialized skills and expertise, using tools and techniques to create, repair, and maintain structures, machinery, and equipment. They contribute to construction projects, manufacturing processes, and the overall infrastructure development of societies.

    Career Guidance

    • 01 Why choose a career?

      Selecting a career is a crucial decision that profoundly influences your life and future prospects. A career goes beyond being just a job, as it offers personal satisfaction, financial stability, and a sense of purpose. Making the right career choice aligns your interests, values, and skills, enabling you to pursue meaningful and rewarding work. A well-chosen career provides avenues for growth, advancement, and professional success. By considering career options that are in-demand and offer competitive salaries, you can ensure both personal fulfillment and financial security.

    • 02 What subjects do I need?

      Selecting the right subjects is crucial for a successful career as it significantly impacts future employment opportunities. Opting for relevant subjects ensures a competitive advantage over others with similar backgrounds and skill sets. Practical experience gained through subject-specific studies makes candidates more desirable to employers during the hiring process. Science and math subjects are particularly valuable as they provide a strong foundation applicable to various fields and increase job prospects. Science enhances understanding of the world and is essential for careers in areas such as research, engineering, and healthcare, while math develops problem-solving skills and numerical competence applicable in financial and analytical roles.
